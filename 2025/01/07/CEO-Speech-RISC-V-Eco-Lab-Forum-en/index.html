CEO Li Weiwei Delivers Speech at RISC-V Ecological Laboratory and Open Source Ecosystem Construction Seminar

2025年01月07日 Kubuds

On January 6, 2025, the “RISC-V Ecological Laboratory and Open Source Ecosystem Construction Seminar,” hosted by Nanjing Yingqi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. and co-organized by Shanghai Kubuds Technology Co., Ltd., was successfully held in the Spring Equinox Conference Room of Nanjing Yingqi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. This seminar brought together numerous industry elites to jointly discuss the construction and development of the RISC-V ecosystem. Kubuds Technology CEO Li Weiwei was invited to attend and delivered an insightful speech titled “QEMU for RISC-V: Current Status, Progress, and Integration Discussion in RISC-V Lab.”

At the seminar, Li Weiwei first expressed sincere gratitude to Nanjing Yingqi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. for the kind invitation. He stated that this seminar provided a good communication platform for the construction and development of the RISC-V ecosystem, allowing all parties to share and discuss experiences and insights in RISC-V ecosystem construction. Meanwhile, he emphasized the important position and contributions of Nanjing Yingqi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. in RISC-V ecosystem construction, especially the remarkable progress made in the construction of projects such as the Nanjing RISC-V Ecosystem Laboratory and the RISC-V Prosperity 2036 Nanjing Drift Base.

Mr. Li Weiwei (1st from the right) delivering the report (1st from the left is Mr. Wu Wei, Manager of the RISC-V Prosperity 2036)

Subsequently, Li Weiwei introduced in detail the current application status of QEMU in the RISC-V architecture. He said that QEMU’s support for RISC-V is now relatively complete, capable of emulating processors of multiple RISC-V architectures and supporting the operation of various operating systems and applications. At the same time, he also pointed out that QEMU still faces challenges and room for improvement in aspects such as performance optimization and function expansion.

When talking about the progress of QEMU, Li Weiwei shared Kubuds Technology’s research achievements and practical experience in this field. He mentioned that Kubuds Technology has improved the performance and stability of QEMU through continuous optimization and improvement. In addition, Kubuds Technology also actively participates in the construction of the open-source community, collaborating with other developers to promote the function expansion and innovation of QEMU.

Li Weiwei also conducted an in-depth discussion on the integration of QEMU for RISC-V with RISC-V Lab. He believed that RISC-V Lab, as an open experimental platform, provides a good environment for the application and promotion of QEMU. At the same time, QEMU provides powerful simulation capabilities for RISC-V Lab, enabling the laboratory to better support the innovation and development of the RISC-V ecosystem.

The successful holding of this seminar has further strengthened the cohesion of the industry in RISC-V ecosystem construction. As an important participant in the RISC-V ecosystem, Kubuds Technology will work together with all parties to promote the vigorous development of the RISC-V ecosystem and help China achieve greater breakthroughs in the global information industry. Meanwhile, as a member of the “RISC-V Prosperity 2036”, Kubuds Technology calls on more enterprises and developers to join this initiative dedicated to the development of the RISC-V ecosystem, and jointly promote the sustainable development and growth of the RISC-V ecosystem.

Kubuds Technology sincerely thanks Nanjing Yingqi Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. for the invitation, and looks forward to carrying out more in-depth cooperation and exchanges with Yingqi Intelligent and other partners in the construction of the RISC-V ecosystem in the future.
