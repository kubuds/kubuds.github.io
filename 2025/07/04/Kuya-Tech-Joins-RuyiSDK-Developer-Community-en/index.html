Kubuds Technology Officially Joins the RuyiSDK Developer Community

2025-07-04 Kubuds

Recently, Kubuds, a company focused on cutting-edge technology solutions, officially announced its joining of the RuyiSDK Developer Community (RuyiSDK.cn).

As a core member of Kubuds Technology‚Äôs open-source projects, Ziyao (@ziyao) will represent the company in the RuyiSDK Community. Kubuds Technology is a technical team focusing on basic software and open chip ecosystems. After joining, it will actively share its technical experience and insights with other community members, promote the improvement of the community‚Äôs overall technical level, and integrate its technical capabilities and services into the RuyiSDK developer ecosystem. RuyiSDK is an open-source suite developed by the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. It is dedicated to providing RISC-V developers with a complete, full-stack, and powerful development toolchain, covering comprehensive support for compilation, debugging, simulation, and more. It is compatible with mainstream RISC-V development boards in the market, offering one-stop services for RISC-V developers. The RuyiSDK Developer Community is a gathering place for RISC-V technology enthusiasts. Kubuds Technology‚Äôs joining will further enrich the technical resource library of the RuyiSDK platform, bringing more diverse choices and a more efficient development experience to developers.
