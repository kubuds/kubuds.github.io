RISC-V China Summit 2024 Concludes Successfully

2024-08-24 Kubuds

On the evening of August 23, the RISC-V China Summit 2024, organized by Shanghai Kubuds Technology Co., Ltd., came to a successful conclusion. This summit brought together the RISC-V International Foundation, industry experts, corporate representatives, and community partners to discuss the latest developments and future trends of RISC-V.

Summary Data

This three-day technical extravaganza included:

145 presentation sessions,

Over 20 exciting concurrent events,

Attracting approximately 3,000 enthusiastic offline participants.

RISC-V Software Porting and Optimization Championship Contestant Exchange Meeting

Hosted by Kubuds Technology’s “Suan Cheng Team” (Cyber Lime Team), this exchange meeting became a gathering point for technology enthusiasts. Mr. Li Cheng, Chief Engineer of Kubuds Technology and Chairperson of the 1st Championship, delivered a wonderful summary of the past two competitions and presented the results of achievement transformation.

The on-site organizing committee and participants jointly used the Milk V Pioneer Box to demonstrate the porting of RetroArch (one of the competition topics) and conducted on-site debug testing, attracting many technology enthusiasts to stop and watch.

KarsierOS and RISC-V Documentation Project Promotion Meeting

Mr. Zhang Xiang, Community Operations Advisor at Kubuds Tech, delivered an exciting sharing session on KarsierOS and the RISC-V Documentation Project.

The current documentation project highlights many difficulties encountered by new users and provides detailed solutions. The overall wiki focuses heavily on the user experience for newcomers, guiding users to better master RISC-V and more effectively use RISC-V products.

The developer experience section includes usage experiences with OpenJDK, even noting that JDK8’s C2 successfully output “hello world” on a Meles board. Our next plan is to add more researched products to enrich our wiki content.

Kubuds Tech’s Comprehensive Participation

At the summit, team members from Kubuds Tech served multiple roles, including organizing committee members, sharing speakers, on-site hosts, and venue management staff.
