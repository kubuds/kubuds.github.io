## About Us

Founded in 2023, Shanghai Kubuds Technology Co., Ltd. is a technical team focusing on basic software and open chip ecology. Team members come from well-known universities such as University of Science and Technology of China and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. They have accumulated many years of experience in basic software fields such as compilers, virtual machines and simulators, and have deep participation and influence on RISC-V open architecture and its open source ecology. The company is committed to providing solid support for domestic enterprises and teams engaged in chip research and development and information product innovation.
