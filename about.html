About Kubuds

About Us

Founded in 2023, Shanghai Kubuds Technology Co., Ltd. is a technical team focusing on foundational software and open chip ecology. We have accumulated more than 10 years of experience in foundational software fields such as compilers, virtual machines and simulators, and have deep participation and influence on RISC-V open architecture and its open source ecology. The company is committed to providing solid support for enterprises and teams engaged in chip research and development and information product innovation.

Vision

Everyone has the opportunity to unleash their creative potential and push the boundaries of human knowledge.

Misson

To provide inventors and entrepreneurs with flexible, convenient and affordable software and hardware innovation environment.

Approach

Around RISC-V and open source software ecosystem, we create a research and development environment for developers to quickly carry out hardware and software collaborative innovation, including chip-assisted design tools, software toolchains, automated analysis tools, etc.
