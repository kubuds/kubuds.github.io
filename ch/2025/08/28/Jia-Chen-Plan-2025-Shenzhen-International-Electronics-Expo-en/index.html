Empowering the "RISC-V Prosperity 2036" at the 2025 Shenzhen International Electronics Expo

2025年08月28日 Kubuds

From August 26-28, 2025, the elexcon 2025 Shenzhen Electronics Expo commenced at the Futian Convention and Exhibition Center. This year’s exhibition theme was “All for AI, All for GREEN,” bringing together global exhibitors to explore the future of the electronics industry. The PLCT Laboratory, in collaboration with Kubuds Technology and several open-source communities, supported the RISC-V Prosperity 2036’s first public appearance at the Shenzhen International Electronics Expo.

About the “RISC-V Prosperity 2036”

The RISC-V Prosperity 2036 is a non-profit community initiative focused on the development of the RISC-V open-source architecture. It is committed to establishing an open standard system and open-source system software stack covering the entire information industry—from data centers to desktop office applications, and from mobile wearables to smart IoT—based on RISC-V before the next Bingchen year (Year of the Dragon, 2036). The goal is to advance the RISC-V software and hardware ecosystem to the maturity level required by a mainstream instruction set architecture.

Kubuds Tech’s Empowering Journey

As one of the member units of the RISC-V Prosperity 2036, Kubuds Tech was responsible for the exhibition arrangements related to the Plan. This included preliminary poster design, printing presentation materials, shipping items, putting up posters, and assigning engineers to be present on-site.

Kubuds Tech Engineers Ziyao (Left) and Sun Min (Right)

Achievement Display

Audience Engagement

The exhibition content included an introduction to the RISC-V Prosperity 2036, the intern recruitment plan, the “Random Drifting Plan” for RISC-V development boards, and an overview of the Nanpan River Joint Plan. The unique exhibition posters and innovative open-source community operation concepts attracted many domestic and international visitors, including college students, chip design enthusiasts, embedded developers, and industry experts. Kubuds engineers had in-depth discussions with the audience about the vision and progress of the RISC-V Prosperity 2036.
