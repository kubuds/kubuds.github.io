CEO Li Weiwei Attends Hefei RISC-V Ecosystem Laboratory and Open-Source Ecosystem Construction Seminar and Delivers a Speech

2025年01月13日 Kubuds

On January 12, 2025, the “RISC-V Ecosystem Laboratory and Open-Source Ecosystem Construction Seminar (2nd Session, 2025)” – hosted by the School of Mathematics, Hefei University of Technology, and co-organized by the PLCT Laboratory – was successfully held in the conference room of the Jade Science and Education Building. Li Weiwei, CEO of Kubuds Technology, was invited to attend the seminar and delivered an online report titled “Current Status and Basic Development of QEMU RISC-V”.

In the report, Mr. Li Weiwei introduced the current status of mainstream RISC-V open-source simulators, especially the development history of QEMU’s support for the RISC-V architecture, and explained in detail QEMU’s current features and basic principles. In addition, Mr. Li Weiwei also looked forward to QEMU’s future applications in the RISC-V field and its development prospects in the community and industry.

Mr. Li Weiwei expressed sincere gratitude to Hefei University of Technology for the invitation and hoped to carry out more in-depth cooperation and exchanges in the future. Kubuds Technology will continue to uphold the concept of open cooperation, work hand in hand with all parties, and strive for the development of the RISC-V ecosystem. This seminar focused on the construction and development of the RISC-V ecosystem, aiming to promote the collaborative innovation of member units and developers and drive the further prosperity of the RISC-V ecosystem. As an important participant in the RISC-V ecosystem, Kubuds Technology has been committed to promoting the application of RISC-V technology in fields such as high-performance computing, artificial intelligence, and the Internet of Things. Recently, Kubuds Technology has achieved certain research and practical results in the field of RISC-V open-source simulators. Through this report, Kubuds Technology demonstrated its professional strength and profound accumulation in the RISC-V technology field, further consolidating its position in the industry.
